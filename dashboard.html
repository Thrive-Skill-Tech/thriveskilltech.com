Frequently Asked Questions
How do I access my course content?+
All course content is available in the Course Content section of this dashboard. Click on any module to expand and view lessons.
When will I get my certificate?+
Certificates are issued after completing all modules and assignments. For MCDM students, the internship certificate is issued after completing the 1-month internship.
How do I submit assignments?+
Submit assignments directly to your mentor Tej Pawar on WhatsApp: +91 84596 12191. Include your name and module number.
Can I get a refund?+
Please contact us within 3 days of enrollment for refund queries. Email tst@thriveskilltech.com or call +91 84596 12191.
How do I join the internship?+
Internship details are shared after completing the main course modules. Your mentor will guide you on the process via WhatsApp.
