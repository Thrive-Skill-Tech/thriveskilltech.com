FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling at Thrive Skill Tech.

What courses does Thrive Skill Tech offer?+
Thrive Skill Tech offers two courses: the CCSC (Certified Content Strategy Course) — a 7-day beginner program at ₹1,999 — and the MCDM (Master in Content Creation and Digital Marketing) — a 2-month flagship course with a 1-month internship at ₹8,999. Both include a certificate of completion.
Is the course 100% online?+
Yes, both courses are fully online. You can learn from anywhere in Maharashtra — Bhandara, Nagpur, Mumbai or any other city. All content, assignments and mentorship happen via WhatsApp and our student dashboard at thriveskilltech.com.
What is the difference between CCSC and MCDM?+
The CCSC is a 7-day beginner course covering content creation basics, monetization tips and practical assignments — ideal if you want to get started quickly. The MCDM is a comprehensive 2-month program covering video creation, editing (CapCut, Premiere Pro), Instagram, YouTube, SEO, Meta Ads, AI tools, personal branding and a 1-month paid internship — ideal for those who want a complete digital career.
Do I get a certificate after completing the course?+
Yes. All students receive a Certificate of Completion after finishing their course modules and assignments. MCDM students also receive an Internship Certificate after completing the 1-month internship — which is our biggest differentiator.
How do I pay? Is it secure?+
Payments are processed via Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. You can pay using UPI, Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking or Wallets. The entire payment is 100% secure and encrypted. You can also contact us on WhatsApp to pay directly.
Who is the mentor? What is their experience?+
Your mentor is Tej Shrikant Pawar — a Content Creation and Digital Marketing practitioner currently working as a Paid IT Intern at Purolic, pursuing B.Tech in Electronics and Computer Engineering and an MS in Computer Science from Woolf University. He teaches practical, real-world skills — not just theory.
What will I learn in the MCDM course?+
The MCDM covers 20 modules including: content creation, niche selection, script writing, video shooting and editing (CapCut, Premiere Pro), Instagram and YouTube growth strategies, Facebook and Instagram Ads, SEO basics, AI tools for creators, personal branding, sales funnels, personality development and a 1-month real-world internship.
Can I get a refund if I am not satisfied?+
Refund requests are accepted within 3 days of enrollment. Contact us at tst2026@thriveskilltech.com or WhatsApp +91 84596 12191 with your enrollment details. After 3 days, refunds are not applicable as course content access is immediately granted.
Is Thrive Skill Tech a registered company?+
Yes. Thrive Skill Tech is an MSME-registered business (Udyam Registration) founded in 2026, based in Bhandara and Mumbai, Maharashtra. We are committed to bringing quality digital education to every student in Maharashtra.
How do I access my course after enrolling?+
After enrollment is confirmed, you will receive login credentials for the Student Dashboard at thriveskilltech.com/dashboard.html. From there you can access all modules, track your progress, view payment status, submit assignments and download your certificate — all in one place.
